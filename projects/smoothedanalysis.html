Research details coming soon! :) In the meantime, check out the basics of smooothed analysis at Wikipedia and, since that Wikipedia article is surprsingly lacking for even the basics, check out one or all of these if interested:

  • Spielman, Daniel A., and Teng, S. "Smoothed Analysis of Algorithms: Why the Simplex Algorithm Usually Takes Polynomial Time" Journal of the ACM 51.3 (2004): 385-463 - Spielman and Teng's original paper that founded smoothed analysis and won the 2008 Gödel Award.
  • Spielman, Daniel A., and Teng, S. "Smoothed Analysis of Algorithms: An Attempt to Explain the Behavior of Algorithms in Practice" Communications of the the ACM Volume 52, Issue 10 (2009): 76-84 - More recent paper by Spielman and Teng, in the form of a discussion-heavy survey.
  • Manthey, B., and Röglin, H. "Smoothed Analysis: Analysis of Algorithms Beyond Worst Case" Information Technology Methoden und innovative Anwendungen der Informatik und Informationstechnik.
