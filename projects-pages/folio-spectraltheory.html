Spectral Theory of Operators Research

Mathematics Research

My research interests in the area of operator theory consist of investigating the spectral theory of Schrödinger operators and the more general problem of the spectral theory of self-adjoint operators. For an overview of my interests in this area, check out Alex Kiselev's great write-up for prospective graduate students: http://math.rice.edu/~ak55/so1.pdf.

If interested in hearing more about this area, feel free to contact me.
