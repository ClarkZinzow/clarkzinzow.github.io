Spectral Theory of Operators Research

Mathematics Research

My research interests in the area of operator theory consist of investigating the spectral theory of Schrödinger operators and the spectral theory of a multitude of linear operators. Some of my other functional analysis flavored endeavors include: Exploring the rigorous mathematical formulation of quantum mechanics and how this formal theory can benefit quantum computation theory and quantum programming language theory; Thinking about some very general open Banach space theory problems.
